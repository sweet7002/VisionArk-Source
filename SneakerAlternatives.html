The Alternatives To Stereotypical Sneakers

In previous years, sneakers such as the Air Force 1s, New Balance 550s, and Nike Dunks Low have been in the limelight, particularly in the summer.


However, in 2024, that doesn’t appear to be the case. With Adidas Sambas and Adidas Campus sneakers taking the spotlight, it seems like this summer will be a classier one.



The price of the Adidas Sambas has increased immensely due to their popularity. Most notably, the English Prime Minister Rishi Sunak has become famous for wearing Sambas in photos.


However, the sneakers can still be found for £75 and upwards. Due to the classier and monotone design of the Sambas, they are incredibly easy to style, especially because of the white colour.



Another shoe that has been gathering attention in the past few years is the Timberland Premium 6. These shoes start at approximately £150 and maintain their price according to statistics.


They have become incredibly popular in the United Kingdom, especially since they provide a similar but more masculine design compared to UGG silhouettes.
